I am a PhD candidate at **University College London** and a PhD Scholar at the **Stone Centre**. I am also a Research Fellow at the **Institute for the Future of Work** and a Research Affiliate at **Holistic AI**.

My [research](/research/) bridges economics and AI, studying how humans and AI co-adapt over time. I use cognitive economics to investigate the alignment and interpretability of AI systems, with a particular focus on bias, fairness, and societal impact.
