Hello!

I am a PhD candidate at University College London and a PhD Scholar at the Stone Centre, with research collaborations with Holistic AI and IFOW.

My research focuses on the intersection of large language models and society, examining issues of bias, fairness, and the societal impact of AI systems.

You can contact me at ze.wang.19@ucl.ac.uk.

  • SAGED: A Holistic Bias-Benchmarking Pipeline for Language Models with Customisable Fairness Calibration Proceedings of the 31st International Conference on Computational Linguistics (COLING)
